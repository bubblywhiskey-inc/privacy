Privacy Policy

Timelines: Life Log  ·  Bubbly Whiskey, Inc.  ·  Effective April 13, 2026

Timelines: Life Log is a personal life-logging app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. All data you enter — events, dates, descriptions, custom fields, and personal info entries — is stored exclusively on your device using a local database.

Data Storage

  • All app data is stored locally on your device.
  • Data is never uploaded to Bubbly Whiskey servers or any third-party servers.
  • Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device.

Over-the-Air Updates

The app uses Expo's update service (expo.dev) to deliver minor app updates without requiring an App Store download. This service may log basic technical information such as your device platform and app version. No personal data from within the app is shared. See Expo's Privacy Policy for details.

Analytics & Advertising

The app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since no data leaves your device, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
